Story Behind 3P Brains

I’m 2019 Batcg MBBS Student at Government Medical College Siddipet. I Was an OCD Patient. Since I left my home for NEET Preparation, Separation from home triggered OCD to me. Till late 2020, I Wasn’t able to understand that I had OCD.

In Starting of 2021, I reached out to Psychiatrist for seeking help. With help of Medicine and therapy, I Recovered from OCD. Then I thought if I Being an MBBS Student I couldn’t figure out my psychological disorder, what about nonmedical background people.

More than 56 million Indians are suffering from Depression. Only one psychiatrist and One Psychologist is available over 1 Lac Indians. WHO estimated economic loss of $1.03 Trillion from 2012-2030 due to mental health issues. After this pandemic, 1 in every 5 is suffering from a mental disorder. Roughly 38 million people suffer from a type of anxiety.

70% OF general physician don’t take mental health into consideration while making a diagnosis. Very often, anxiety is misdiagnosed with heart problems. Every year 2,00,000 Indians take their lives. The statistics are even higher if one starts to include the number of attempts to suicide. 40% people are not aware about their psychiatric illness, however they’re ready to take treatment if explained to them. Main three Psychiatric illness are – OCD, Depression, Anxiety. 70-80% of patients can be treated with Psychotherapy known as CBT and rest 20- 30% requires medicin
